About Us

A no-frills roadside stop with deep local roots.

Bill's Hot Dogs in Greenville is known for its bright red hot dogs, quick service, and a signature style built around mustard, chopped onions, and spicy chili. Most descriptions of the brand trace the Bill's story back to founder Bill Jackson, who opened the original stand in Washington, North Carolina in 1928.

The site leans into that old-school feel while giving customers the practical details they usually need first: hours, menu highlights, phone number, and directions. It is designed to work cleanly on phones, where most local restaurant traffic starts.
